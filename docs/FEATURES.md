# TripCompare — Feature Overview

> India's first group travel aggregator. Compare trips, book with confidence, travel with peace of mind.

---

## For Travelers

### Find & Compare Trips
- Browse group trips by destination, type (adventure, trekking, beach, road trip, etc.)
- Filter by price, dates, group size, and ratings
- **Compare up to 3 trips side-by-side** — pricing, itinerary, inclusions, organizer ratings
- View destination pages with all upcoming trips for that location

### Book Your Spot
- **Instant booking** — pay and confirm in one click
- **Request-based booking** — request a spot, organizer approves, then you pay
- Fill in traveler details (name, age, gender, ID proof)
- **Choose your seat** — interactive seat map shows available seats across all vehicles
- Early bird pricing — book early, pay less
- Apply wallet balance to reduce payment amount

### Payments & Protection
- Pay securely via Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking)
- **Money held via SafePay** — organizer gets paid only after the trip is done
- Cancel anytime — refund based on cancellation policy (flexible / moderate / strict)
- Refunds go to your in-app wallet instantly

### Chat with Organizer
- Direct chat with the trip organizer before and after booking
- **Anti-leakage protection** — phone numbers, UPI IDs, emails, and links are blocked in chat (keeps transactions on-platform)
- Real-time messaging with typing indicators and read receipts

### Wallet & Cashback
- All refunds credited to wallet instantly
- **Earn cashback** on completed trips (issued by admin)
- Use wallet balance on future bookings

### Stay Updated
- **In-app notifications** — booking confirmations, payment updates, trip changes, organizer responses
- Unread badge on notification bell so you never miss anything

### Reviews
- Rate trips on 5 dimensions — overall, organization, value, safety, accuracy
- Add photos and comments
- Read other travelers' reviews before booking

---

## For Organizers

### Create & Manage Trips
- Rich trip creation — photos, day-wise itinerary, inclusions/exclusions, pricing
- Set pickup & drop points, group size limits, cancellation policy
- **Draft → Publish** workflow — prepare everything, then go live
- Edit trips anytime with full edit history (audit trail)
- Toggle bookings on/off without changing trip status

### Vehicle & Seat Management
- **Add multiple vehicles** per trip (sedan, Innova, tempo traveller, minibus, etc.)
- **Visual seat layout builder** — pick a vehicle template, customize the grid
- See real-time seat status — who's booked where, which seats are available
- Seat selection integrates directly into the booking flow for travelers

### Booking & Request Management
- View all bookings for each trip with traveler details
- **Approve/reject** booking requests (for request-based trips)
- See payment status per booking
- Dashboard with stats — total trips, bookings, revenue

### Payments & Earnings
- Detailed payment history per trip
- **SafePay protection** — funds released automatically after trip completion (90-day safety buffer)
- Revenue summaries and per-trip breakdowns

### Chat & Reviews
- Chat with travelers directly on the platform
- Reply to reviews publicly

## For Admins (Platform Team)

### Dashboard & Analytics
- **Platform overview** — total users, organizers, trips, bookings, revenue
- Visual charts — revenue trend (line), booking distribution (bar), trip types (pie)

### Organizer Management
- **Approval queue** — review and approve/reject organizer applications
- Organizers get notified instantly on approval/rejection

### Booking Oversight
- Search and filter all bookings across the platform
- Drill into any booking — traveler details, payment transactions

### Cashback Management
- View completed trips eligible for cashback
- **Issue cashback** per traveler per trip (with duplicate prevention and amount cap)
- Track cashback history by user or by trip

### Moderation
- **Flagged messages** — review chat messages flagged by the anti-leakage filter
- Close conversations if needed
- Full payment dashboard across the platform

## Behind the Scenes (What Makes It Reliable)

- **SafePay payments** — traveler money is safe until the trip is done
- **Auto trip lifecycle** — trips automatically move from Active → Full → Completed
- **Seat holds expire** — if you don't pay within the window, seats are released for others
- **Stale booking cleanup** — unpaid bookings auto-expire so seats aren't blocked forever
- **Real-time everything** — chat, notifications, seat availability update live
- **Mobile-first design** — works great on phones, tablets, and desktops
